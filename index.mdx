# Documentation

The **Mosaic Platform** by **Transmit Security** secures and simplifies the full customer journey with unified customer identity management, fraud prevention and identity verification services. With a powerful risk engine and layers of orchestrated services, the platform adapts user flows to mitigate risk or elevate trust in real time. Explore the docs to learn everything you need to power your applications with Mosaic.
